# Problem Statement & SMART Goals

## Problem Statement
Manual identity and access management across revenue cycle operations results in inconsistent access decisions, slow onboarding/offboarding, and audit gaps for HIPAA and privacy requirements.

## SMART Goals
1. Reduce new-hire provisioning time to **< 8 hours** within **90 days** of go-live.
2. Remove high-risk access within **1 hour** of termination for **95%** of leavers within **60 days**.
3. Achieve **>95%** access certification completion by due date each quarter.
4. Produce a standardized evidence pack within **2 business days** during audits.
