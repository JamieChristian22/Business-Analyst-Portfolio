# Epics (Agile)

- **E-01 Identity Lifecycle (JML):** Joiner/Mover/Leaver workflows from HR events  
- **E-02 Access Request Catalog:** catalog, forms, routing, approvals, fulfillment  
- **E-03 RBAC Role Model:** business roles mapped to entitlements  
- **E-04 Access Certifications:** quarterly high-risk, monthly privileged  
- **E-05 SoD & Risk Policies:** prevent conflicts + exception workflow  
- **E-06 Reporting & Audit Evidence:** SLA reporting + evidence export  
- **E-07 UAT/Training/Cutover:** testing, enablement, hypercare support
