### 1. SaaS Business Analyst Case Study  
**Focus**: Optimizing customer onboarding for a SaaS platform through CRM integration, automation, and KPI tracking.  

**Problem Statement**  
- Onboarding averaged **21 days** (target: 14).  
- High churn rate (**15%**) in first 90 days.  
- Fragmented tools & poor inter-team visibility.  

**Scope of Work**  
- Map As-Is/To-Be workflows.  
- Gather requirements from Sales, Implementation, and Customer Success.  
- Analyze CRM + support ticket data.  
- Define KPIs and dashboard reporting.

### 2. Healthcare Business Analyst Case Study  
**Focus**: Reducing manual delays in claims management through workflow redesign and SLA-based automation.  

**Problem Statement**  
- Manual claim reviews created bottlenecks.  
- Lack of SLA tracking = long turnaround times.  
- Low provider/patient satisfaction.  

**Scope of Work**  
- Document As-Is claims workflow.  
- Interview Claims, Compliance, IT stakeholders.  
- Perform gap analysis on processing time.  
- Recommend automation & SLA alerts.

### 3. Coca-Cola Business Analyst Case Study  
**Focus**: Enhancing supply chain visibility and KPI reporting across Coca-Cola distribution.  

**Problem Statement**  
- Lack of real-time inventory visibility.  
- No centralized tracking of OTIF (On-Time In-Full).  
- Stockouts occurring without early warnings.  

**Scope of Work**  
- Map data flows between Manufacturing, Logistics, Retail.  
- Define supply chain KPIs.  
- Recommend dashboard framework.
